🏠 Household Management

Chore Tracking That Actually Works

Assign, track, and verify every household chore in real time. Tasks are visible to the whole house so nobody can claim ignorance. Submit photo proof for bounties, earn points for completions, and watch the leaderboard shift with every dish done.

Personal Growth, Fully Private

Track your own 21-day habits — gym, sleep, hydration, reading — in your Private Chambers. Your progress, consistency scores, and streaks are 100% encrypted and invisible to the rest of the house. Earn bonus XP for yourself, silently.
