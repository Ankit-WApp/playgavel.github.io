Core Gameplay

Welcome to The Court

⚖️

Rotating Judge

Every week a new Judge is crowned with full authority — set laws, assign bounties, approve completions, and sentence slackers.

📜

Set The Law

Create permanent or temporary household rules. No dishes? No screens after 9pm? The Judge's word is law until next week.

🎯

Bounty System

Place massive point rewards on the tasks nobody wants. High-risk bounties that can flip the entire leaderboard overnight.

🏦

Family Bank

The Judge controls tax rates, pooling earnings into the Family Bank to fund shared household rewards everyone votes on.

📸

Photo Verdicts

Submit photo proof for bounties. The Judge reviews the evidence and approves or rejects — keeping the court honest.

🏆

Reward Vault

Cash hard-earned points for real household privileges — TV rights, thermostat control, or custom rewards your house cares about.

📊

Leaderboard

Weekly, Monthly, and All-Time standings keep the pressure on. Track who's grinding and who's hiding in the Doghouse.

⚡

Real-Time Activity

Instant notifications for card plays, Doghouse sentences, bounty claims, and every dramatic courtroom moment.

🧠

Habit Tracking

21-day private habit missions with consistency scores. Earn bonus XP for streaks — all locked away from your housemates.

Consequence System
🏠 In The Doghouse

Fair. Brutal. Avoidable.

The Doghouse keeps everyone accountable without anyone having to be the bad guy. The app is the judge — you just do the time.

💸

Higher Taxes

Land in the Doghouse and pay elevated taxes on everything you earn until you prove your commitment to the court.

🙏

Plea The Judge

🃏
