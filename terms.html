02Description of Service

Gavel is a gamified household management application that allows users to:

  • Create and manage household tasks and personal habits
  • Earn and spend house points
  • Use power cards with various effects to strategize and level up on the leaderboard
  • Participate in a rotating Judge system and rule the house
  • Trade tasks and power cards on a marketplace
  • Track streaks and achievements
